# Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** June 2026

## Who we are

This website is a personal project. For any privacy-related questions, you can contact us at: a.trevisan@proton.me

## Data we collect

When you create an account, we collect:

- **Email address** — used for login
- **Username** — chosen by you at registration
- **Quiz scores** — the results of the quizzes you have completed

We do not collect any other data. We do not use tracking cookies, we do not integrate analytics tools, and we do not share your data with third parties for advertising purposes.

## How we use your data

Your data is used exclusively to:

- allow you to access your account
- save your scores and display your progress

## Who we share your data with

Data is stored on **Supabase** (supabase.com), the database service we use. Supabase processes data in compliance with GDPR. We do not share your data with any other party.

## How long we keep your data

Your data is retained for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, all associated data is permanently deleted.

## Your rights

As a user, you have the right to:

- **access** your personal data
- **correct** inaccurate data
- **delete** your account and all associated data (you can do this directly from your profile settings)
- **object** to processing or request its limitation

To exercise these rights, write to: a.trevisan@proton.me

## Changes to this policy

If we make significant changes to this policy, we will notify you by email or through a notice on the website.
